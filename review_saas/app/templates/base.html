About This Project – ReviewSaaS

ReviewSaaS uses Sentiment Analysis to automatically classify customer feedback as positive, negative, or neutral—helping teams understand emotions, trends, drivers of satisfaction, and recurring issues. It assists with:

  • Aggregating multi-platform reviews in one place
  • Tracking sentiment over time
  • Highlighting top themes and pain points
  • Export-ready dashboards for stakeholders
